frequently asked questions

how long do aoyn press-on nails last?

with proper application and care, our nails can last 7-14 days per wear. they're also reusable, just gently remove, clean, and reapply.

are the nails reusable?

yes! all aoyn sets are designed to be reused. just remove them carefully and store them in the original packaging.

what comes in each nail set?

each set includes 24 nails in multiple sizes, nail glue, adhesive tabs, a mini file, cuticle stick, and application instructions.

do i have to use nail glue?

nope! you can use the included adhesive tabs for a short-term, no-damage option. nail glue gives a longer-lasting hold.

will press-ons damage my natural nails?

not if removed correctly! we recommend soaking your nails in warm, soapy water before gently lifting them off, never force them.

do you offer custom designs or sizing?

at the moment, we don't offer custom designs, but we're always working on expanding our collections. stay tuned!

how do i find the right size?

our 24-piece sets include a wide range of sizes for a perfect fit. we also provide a sizing guide to help you measure your natural nails.

where do you ship, and how long does it take?

we currently ship in malta only! local orders typically arrive in 3-7 business days. international shipping is unavailable at this moment.
