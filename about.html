welcome to aoyn – your go-to destination for beautiful press-on nails.

at aoyn, we believe everyone deserves gorgeous nails, without the time of a salon visit.

founded in 2025 by certified nail tech kaylee borg, aoyn was born from a passion for effortless style and an alternative to permanence in manicures.

kaylee set out to create high-quality, reusable press-on nails that actually look and feel like the real deal. no gluey mess or cheap plastic here!

our mission is simple: to empower confidence through effortless beauty. whether you're heading to brunch, a big event, or just want to treat yourself, we’ve got styles to match every mood, vibe, and outfit.

what makes us different?

each aoyn set is handcrafted with love and designed to last. we use salon-grade materials for a chip-resistant finish and a comfortable, natural fit. from classic nudes to bold statement designs, our nails are made to elevate your everyday look in minutes.

quality first.

we stand by the quality of our products; no shortcuts, no compromises. all of our nails are cruelty-free, non-toxic, and carefully tested for wear and durability. we’re proud to offer reusable sets that are as easy on your wallet as they are on the planet.

our community.

we’re more than just nails, we’re a growing community of beauty lovers who believe in self-expression, self-care, and showing up as your boldest self. we love seeing how you style our press-ons, and we’re always working to bring you the latest trends and most requested designs.

join the aoyn life!

thank you for being here. whether you're a first-time customer or a loyal fan, we’re so glad you’re part of our journey. here’s to strong nails, strong women, and endless style.
